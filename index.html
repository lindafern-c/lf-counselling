Linda Fern Counselling

My name is Linda Fern and I'm a qualified accredited Integrative Counsellor and Psychotherapist operating from Mansfield, Nottinghamshire & Derby. My counselling style incorporates a Person-centred approach, Attachment and Cognitive Behavioural Therapy. I have over 18 years of experience and can provide counselling and psychotherapy for a wide range of issues for both adults and young people.

If you would like to know more about my practice or therapeutic approach,
please get in touch to arrange a free 20-minute telephone consultation.
I offer in-person appointments at Lotus Wellbeing Centre in Mansfield and
Being Psychotherapy in Derby, as well as online and telephone sessions, if preferred.
